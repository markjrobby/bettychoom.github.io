Lennylemons

Content Creation, Social Media Management, Facebook Ads, Email Marketing

I've worked with Lennylemons, an online children clothing store since December 2016. As the social media manager, I provide daily social media content such as mom hacks, funny quotes, memes and videos on their Facebook page and Instagram. The social platforms were inconsistent and I overhauled their social media presence by creating a brand design throughout every post. I also manage their Facebook Ads, designing the graphics and copy for different ad campaigns to achieve higher reach and sales.

Email marketing is another aspect of my work. Using MailChimp, I use the client's content and images such as new clothing arrivals and customers' photos to craft a copy in Lennylemons’ upbeat mom's voice. The newsletter is sent out to thousands of subscribers and has an open rate of around 30% – double the industry average of 15%.

Other parts of my jobscope in the beginning included customer service and bloggers collaboration. Customer service is an important component for Lennylemons and I worked to a 1-hour response period, ensuring customer queries are replied to efficiently. Besides that, I was also tasked to reach out to mom bloggers to get Lennylemons' products featured on their Instagram/blogs.

Basho and Friends

Basho and Friends is a YouTube channel, creating original musical content for kids to learn different languages like Spanish, French and Mandarin. They are one of my very first clients and I was the sole social media manager for the brand.

I was responsible for all aspects of Basho and Friends’ social media presence across Facebook, Twitter, Google+ and Pinterest. I created relevant, shareable content, interacted with users and encouraged engagement on these platforms. Since June 2016, the page's likes has increased by 20% and the reach has increased to an average of 400% organically. To reach out to a wider audience, I also joined several language learning Facebook Groups and promoted Basho and Friends' videos and YouTube channel on them.

Another part of my role included monthly e-newsletters. The newsletter works as a round-up of the month's activities such as updates, special annoucements, new video uploads and information. Using the client's images and videos, I edited them into something short and fun, crafting the copy in Basho and Friends' casual voice. The newsletter is sent out to thousands of subscribers and has an open rate of around 24%.
